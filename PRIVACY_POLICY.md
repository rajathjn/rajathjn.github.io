# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how GopherUpload ("we," "our," or "the software") handles information when you use our YouTube video upload tool.

## Information Collection

### Data We Access

GopherUpload may access the following information solely for the purpose of uploading videos to YouTube:

- **YouTube Account Information**: Through OAuth 2.0 authentication, we access your YouTube account to upload videos on your behalf.
- **Video Files**: The software processes video files you choose to upload from your local device.
- **Video Metadata**: Title, description, tags, and other metadata you provide for your uploads.

### Data We Store Locally

- **OAuth Tokens**: Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device (`youtube-token.json`) to maintain your YouTube session.
- **Configuration Settings**: Your preferences are stored locally in `config.json`.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information
- We do **not** transmit your data to any third-party servers
- We do **not** store your videos or metadata on external servers
- We do **not** track your usage or analytics
- We do **not** have access to your YouTube password

## Third-Party Services

GopherUpload interacts with the following third-party services:

- **YouTube Data API v3**: Used to upload videos to your YouTube channel. Your use of this feature is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [YouTube's Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms).

## Data Security

- All authentication is handled through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol
- Your credentials are never stored or transmitted by this software
- All data remains on your local device

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Revoke the application's access to your YouTube account at any time through [Google Security Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
- Delete locally stored tokens and configuration files
- Stop using the software at any time

## Children's Privacy

This software is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
